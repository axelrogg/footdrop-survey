What is this?

This project collects real-world experiences from people living with foot drop to better understand its causes, impact, and current treatment limitations. The goal is to support research and the development of more effective, patient-centered solutions.

  • Takes under 10 minutes.
  • Completely anonymous.
  • No medical records required.
  • Your data will never ever be shared and/or sold.

Why this project exists?

I've lived with foot drop for several years and experienced firsthand how limited and inconsistent current solutions can be. As an electrical engineering student, I started exploring whether technology could offer better solutions than what currently exists.

Then I realized maybe someone else might benefit from my own solution. But foot drop syndrome manifests differently depending on the causes. So now I'm researching how technology can better support people living with this condition by grounding future work in real patient experiences.
